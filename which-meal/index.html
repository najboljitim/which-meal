WhichMeal.com has been designed for world wayfarers interested in real traditional food and travel, in culinary experimentation. It is a gourmet traveler's essential digital companion which will finally tell you what to eat in the most famous food destinations in the world: France, China, Italy, Thailand, Japan... but also in some lesser know delicious countries.

So that when wandering across new regions of the globe, we'll escort you through your food journey and we'll make sure you don't miss out on delicious cuisines and wonderful recipes chefs around you have to offer.

Ambassador program

We believe cuisine(s) of the world are astounding. We believe travel in itself in one of the greatest reward for anyone. When you mix the two together, a magic combination brings wonderful memories, opens your mind to new horizons and develops your culinary sense. Here is the essence of the reason we exist.

We also believe hundreds people with the combined knowledge of a community is unbeatable. You might be an expert on your country's gastronomy, or you might know the culinary landscape of your city more than any of your friends, and where is this backstreet eatery nobody else knows. You can see things we don’t. But most important than anything else, you know what great cuisine tastes like, and we need people privileged with unique tastes buds like you to be our guides.
